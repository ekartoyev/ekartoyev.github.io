Why Epsilon?

Initially, the idea was to call it e-Notes (electronic notes). But when I saw there were myriads of e-Notes swarming around in Google Play and Internet like bees, I decided to call it Îµ-Notes (or Epsilon Notes).

Epsilon is the Greek letter "e" and the word "electronic" is of Greek origin. Therefore, it felt legitimate to say Epsilon Notes, meaning electronic notes or e-Notes.
